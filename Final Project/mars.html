Planet Profile: Mars

Mars

  • Planet Type: Terrestrial
  • Distance from Sun: 142 million miles (1.52 AU)
  • Year Length: 687 Earth Days
  • Number of Moons: 2 (Phobos & Deimos)

About Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is commonly referred to as the "Red Planet." The reddish appearance is caused by iron oxide (rust) on its surface.

Mars is a cold desert world with a very thin atmosphere. It is home to Olympus Mons, the largest volcano and highest known mountain in the solar system, and Valles Marineris, one of the largest canyons.
