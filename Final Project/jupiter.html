Planet Profile: Jupiter

Jupiter

  • Planet Type: Gas Giant
  • Distance from Sun: 484 million miles (5.2 AU)
  • Year Length: 11.8 Earth Years
  • Number of Moons: 95

About Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and by far the largest in the Solar System. It is more than twice as massive as all the other planets combined.

Jupiter's familiar stripes and swirls are actually cold, windy clouds of ammonia and water floating in an atmosphere of hydrogen and helium. It is famous for its Great Red Spot, a gigantic storm that is larger than Earth and has raged for hundreds of years.
