Planet Profile: Mercury

Mercury

  • Planet Type: Terrestrial
  • Distance from Sun: 36 million miles (0.39 AU)
  • Year Length: 88 Earth Days
  • Number of Moons: 0

About Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in the Solar System and the closest to the Sun. Despite being the closest planet to the Sun, it is not the hottest—that title belongs to Venus.

Because it has almost no atmosphere to retain heat, Mercury experiences extreme temperature fluctuations. Its daytime temperatures can reach a scorching 800 degrees Fahrenheit, while its nighttime temperatures can plummet to negative 290 degrees.
