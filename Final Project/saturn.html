Planet Profile: Saturn

Saturn

  • Planet Type: Gas Giant
  • Distance from Sun: 886 million miles (9.5 AU)
  • Year Length: 29.4 Earth Years
  • Number of Moons: 146

About Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System. It is a gas giant with an average radius of about nine and a half times that of Earth.

Adorned with a dazzling system of icy rings, Saturn is unique among the planets. While other planets have rings, none are as spectacular or as complex as Saturn's, which are made of chunks of ice and rock varying in size from a speck of dust to a house.
