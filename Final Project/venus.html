Planet Profile: Venus

Venus

  • Planet Type: Terrestrial
  • Distance from Sun: 67 million miles (0.72 AU)
  • Year Length: 225 Earth Days
  • Number of Moons: 0

About Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is sometimes called Earth's "sister planet" because of their similar size, mass, and proximity to the Sun. However, that is where the similarities end.

Venus has a thick, toxic atmosphere filled with carbon dioxide, and it is shrouded in thick, yellowish clouds of sulfuric acid. This atmosphere traps heat in a runaway greenhouse effect, making it the hottest planet in our solar system with surface temperatures hot enough to melt lead.
