Planet Profile: Neptune

Neptune

  • Planet Type: Ice Giant
  • Distance from Sun: 2.8 billion miles (30.1 AU)
  • Year Length: 165 Earth Years
  • Number of Moons: 16

About Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest-known Solar planet from the Sun. It is the only planet in our solar system not visible to the naked eye and was the first predicted by mathematics before its discovery in 1846.

Dark, cold, and whipped by supersonic winds, Neptune is an ice giant with a thick atmosphere composed of hydrogen, helium, and methane. Like Uranus, the methane gas absorbs red light and reflects blue light, giving the planet its vivid azure hue.
